# 📊 Ecommerce Sales Dashboard (Tableau)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Ecommerce Sales Dashboard** built using Tableau.
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, customer purchasing behavior, and regional distribution of revenue.

It is designed to help stakeholders quickly identify trends, top-performing categories, and potential areas for growth.

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze overall business performance using key metrics
* Identify high-performing categories and products
* Understand monthly sales trends and seasonality
* Explore geographic distribution of sales
* Evaluate customer purchasing patterns



## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Tableau** – Data visualization & dashboard creation
* **Excel** – Data cleaning and preprocessing

* **Total Profit:** $79.71M

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 🟢 Overall Performance

* The business generated **$533.67M in sales** with **$79.71M in profit**, indicating strong performance.
* A total of **5,000 orders** resulted in **14,963 units sold**, showing multi-item purchasing behavior.



### 📈 Sales Trends

* Sales remained consistent throughout the year, averaging **$43M–$46M monthly**.
* **May recorded the highest sales ($50.76M)**, indicating a peak period.
* Lower sales were observed in **February and September**, suggesting seasonal dips.



### 🛍️ Category Insights

* **Home Decor** is the leading category with **$57.23M in sales**.
* Other strong categories include **Books, Kitchen, and Sports**.
* Categories like **Beauty and Toys** show relatively lower performance.



### 📦 Sub-Category Insights

* Top-performing products include:

  * **Sofa ($2.30M)**
  * **Lamp ($2.25M)**
  * **Bed and Headphones**
* Demand is spread across multiple products, indicating diversified customer preferences.



### 🌍 Regional Insights

* Top-performing states generate sales exceeding **$14M**.
* Several states fall in the **$6M–$7M range**, indicating stable demand.
* Lower-performing regions present opportunities for market expansion.



### 📊 Product Demand

* Product sales are closely distributed (range: **96–122 units**), showing balanced demand.
* No single product dominates significantly.



## 💡 Business Implications

* Strong performance in home-related products suggests a key revenue driver.
* Stable monthly sales indicate a resilient business model.
* Opportunities exist in:

  * Improving low-performing categories
  * Expanding in underperforming regions
  * Leveraging peak sales periods for campaigns



## 🚀 Conclusion

This dashboard highlights how data visualization can transform raw data into actionable insights.
It demonstrates the ability to analyze business performance, identify trends, and support decision-making using Tableau.
